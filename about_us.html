About Us

Celebrating the Essence of Indian Classical Music

At Swar Shiksha, we are dedicated to preserve and promote the rich heritage of Indian Classical Music, a timeless art form that transcends generations. Our platform is a labor of love, born out of a profound appreciation for the intricate melodies, rhythms, and cultural depth that define this musical tradition.

Our Aim

"Swar Shiksha" is an innovative project aimed at creating a user-friendly website tailored to the needs of beginners in Indian Classical Music. This website serves as an accessible gateway for aspiring learners to embark on their musical journey with confidence. It provides basic yet comprehensive introduction to Hindustani vocal music, offering simplified notation systems, introductory lessons, and a wealth of resources to demystify this intricate musical tradition.
